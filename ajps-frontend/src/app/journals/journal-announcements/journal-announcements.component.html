Crossref Membership Approved

Date: November 6, 2021

Details: We are thrilled to announce that Plants and Ecosystem has been officially accepted as a member of Crossref. This milestone enhances our ability to assign Digital Object Identifiers (DOIs) to our publications, ensuring greater visibility and accessibility for our research articles.

Launch of Volume 1, Issue 1

Date: October 10, 2021

Details: Plants and Ecosystem proudly published its inaugural issue, Volume 1, Issue 1, on October 10, 2021. This issue marks the beginning of our journey to disseminate cutting-edge research in plant sciences and ecosystem studies to a global audience.
