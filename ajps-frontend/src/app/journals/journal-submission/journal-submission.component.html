How It Works

Submitting your manuscript is straightforward. Follow these simple steps to get started:

  • Create an Account: Register on our submission portal or log in if you already have an account.
  • Prepare Your Manuscript: Ensure your submission adheres to our Author Guidelines for formatting and content requirements.
  • Upload Your Files: Submit your manuscript, figures, and any supplementary materials through the portal.
  • Track Progress: Monitor your submission’s status, respond to reviewer comments, and communicate with the editorial team.
