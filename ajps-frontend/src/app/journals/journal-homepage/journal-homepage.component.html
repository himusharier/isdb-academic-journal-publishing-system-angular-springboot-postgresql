About the Journal

Plants and Ecosystem is a prestigious, open-access, peer-reviewed journal dedicated to advancing research in plant sciences and ecosystem studies. We welcome contributions from all branches of these fields, fostering rapid dissemination of cutting-edge scientific knowledge to researchers, academics, and enthusiasts worldwide. Published annually, our journal prioritizes innovative and impactful findings while also considering fundamental research with significant scientific merit. Each submission undergoes rigorous peer review to ensure the highest standards of quality and integrity.

Publication Frequency

One issue per year, with the flexibility to adjust based on the volume of high-quality submissions.

Manuscript Categories

We publish a diverse range of works to cater to various research needs:

Original Research Articles: Showcase your groundbreaking research with scientific excellence.

Review Articles: Provide in-depth analyses of contemporary issues or specific research questions.

Short Communications: Share novel, significant findings in a concise format.

Case Reports: Present detailed studies from any science and technology discipline.

Note: We encourage concise manuscripts without strict page or word limits. Visit our Author Guidelines for details.

Aims and Scope

Our journal encompasses a broad spectrum of plant sciences and ecosystem research, including studies involving photosynthetic organisms as model species. Key areas of focus include:

  • Plant Taxonomy
  • Plant Tissue Culture
  • Mycology and Plant Pathology
  • Ecology, Ecosystems, and Evolution
  • Algae Biotechnology and Limnology
  • Genetics, Cytogenetics, and Cell Biology
  • Plant Breeding and Molecular Biology
  • Plant Physiology and Biochemistry
  • Ethnobotany and Economic Botany
  • Phycology and Aquatic Plants
  • Horticulture and Agronomy

Note: Exceptional submissions from other bioscience fields with a strong focus on plants or plant components may also be considered.

Our Editorial Team

The editorial team comprises the Editor-in-Chief, Executive Editor, Advisory Editorial Members, and Editorial Members, supported by a Managing Editor and Editorial Assistant. Appointments last three years, with the possibility of renewal. Selection criteria include expertise, a strong Google Scholar H-index, and relevant experience. The Editor-in-Chief must be an active professor at a reputable university with at least two years of editorial experience. Other members require a doctorate or over five years of research experience. We strive for geographical diversity and comprehensive expertise to maintain a balanced and dynamic team.

Funding and Sustainability

Published by ScholarPress, the journal sustains its operations through an Article Processing Charge (APC) applied to accepted manuscripts. This ensures the journal remains freely accessible to readers worldwide while covering essential maintenance costs.
