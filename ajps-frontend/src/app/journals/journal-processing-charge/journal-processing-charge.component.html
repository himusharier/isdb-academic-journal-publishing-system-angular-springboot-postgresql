Discounts and Waivers

Low-Income Country Discount: Authors from countries classified as low-income or lower-middle-income by the World Bank are eligible for a 50% discount on the standard APC.

Waiver Policy: In exceptional cases, authors facing financial hardship may apply for a full or partial waiver. Waiver requests must be submitted during manuscript submission, including a justification. Decisions are made on a case-by-case basis by the editorial board.

Payment Process

The APC is payable only after a manuscript is accepted for publication following peer review. Authors will receive an invoice with payment instructions, including options for bank transfer, credit card, or institutional payment. No fees are required during the submission or review stages.

For detailed payment instructions, please contact our editorial office at editorial@example.com.

Why an APC?

The APC enables us to:

  • Maintain a rigorous peer-review process.
  • Provide immediate open access to all published articles.
  • Support indexing in major databases like Crossref, Scopus, and DOAJ.
  • Cover operational costs, including website hosting and manuscript management systems.
